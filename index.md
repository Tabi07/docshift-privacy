# Privacy Policy for DocShift

**Effective Date:** 7/4/2026
**Last Updated:** 7/3/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DocShift ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. DocShift is a free utility app that converts PDF files to Word format and Word files to PDF format.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is (and is not) collected, and how it is used.

---

## 1. No Account Required

DocShift does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal login credentials to use the App. You can use all core features of the App without providing any personal information to us.

## 2. Your Documents Stay on Your Device

DocShift performs all PDF-to-Word and Word-to-PDF conversions **locally, on your device**. Your files are:

- **Never uploaded** to any server operated by us or any third party.
- **Never viewed, accessed, stored, or transmitted** by us.
- Processed entirely offline within the App using on-device conversion technology.

Because your documents never leave your device, we have no access to their content, and we do not collect, store, or share any information contained within the files you convert.

## 3. Permissions We Use

To function, DocShift may request the following device permission:

- **Storage / File Access:** Required so the App can read the file you select for conversion and save the converted output back to your device. This access is used solely to perform the conversion locally and is not used to collect or transmit file data to us.

You can manage or revoke app permissions at any time through your device's Settings.

## 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

DocShift is free to use and supported by advertisements served through **Google AdMob**, a third-party advertising service provided by Google.

To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and process certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

- Advertising ID (a resettable, non-permanent device identifier)
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- General location information (such as country or region, derived from IP address)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly, and is governed by Google's own privacy practices. You can learn more about how Google collects and uses this data here:

- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Your Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized/interest-based advertising by:

- Adjusting your **Ads Settings** in your Google Account ([https://adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com))
- Enabling **"Opt out of Ads Personalization"** in your Android device settings (Settings → Google → Ads)
- Resetting your Advertising ID at any time via device settings

## 5. Children's Privacy

DocShift is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads shown in the App are served in accordance with Google AdMob's policies, and we do not target ads specifically at children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it, though as noted above, we do not collect personal information through the App in the first place.

## 6. Data We Do Not Collect

To be clear, DocShift does **not**:

- Collect or store the content of your files
- Require or collect your name, email, or any login credentials
- Track your precise (GPS) location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- Sell your personal information to third parties

## 7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into DocShift is **Google AdMob**, described in Section 4. We do not use any other analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs at this time. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

## 8. Data Security

Since your documents are processed entirely on your device and never transmitted to us, there is no risk of your file content being exposed through our systems. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and the App updated to benefit from the latest security protections.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws (such as the GDPR in the EU/UK or the CCPA in California) regarding any data collected by third-party services like AdMob. Since we do not collect personal data directly, requests related to advertising data should be directed to Google via the links provided in Section 4. For any questions about this policy, you can also contact us using the details below.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App's functionality or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
