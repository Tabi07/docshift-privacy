Privacy Policy for Crypto Crush

Last updated: July 23, 2026 Version: 1.0

Crypto Crush ("we", "our", or "the app") is a puzzle game with a crypto-themed visual style. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

No Account or Login Required

Crypto Crush does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal registration information to use the app.

No Data Collection by the App

Crypto Crush does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you. Specifically, the app itself does not collect:

Names, email addresses, or contact information
Photos, contacts, or location data
Payment or financial information
Any personally identifiable information
In-Game Virtual Currency

Crypto Crush includes a fictional in-game currency ("CrushCoins") awarded for completing levels. CrushCoins:

Have no real-world monetary value
Are not real cryptocurrency
Cannot be exchanged, transferred, withdrawn, redeemed, or converted into real money, Bitcoin, or any other cryptocurrency
Are not connected to any blockchain, wallet, or exchange
Third-Party Services (Advertising)

Crypto Crush uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. To serve ads, AdMob and its partners may automatically collect certain information from your device, such as:

Advertising ID (a resettable, non-permanent device identifier)
IP address
Device information (model, operating system version)
General location (derived from IP address)
Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly, and is used to serve and measure advertising, and in some cases for ad personalization, in accordance with Google's own privacy practices.

You can learn more about how Google collects and uses this data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You may be able to opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (e.g., Android Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization") or through Google's Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

Children's Privacy

Crypto Crush does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If our app is directed to a mixed audience that may include children, ads served through Google AdMob are configured to comply with applicable child-directed advertising requirements (such as the Google Play Families Policy and COPPA, where applicable).

Data Security

Since Crypto Crush does not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal user data for us to secure. Any data handled by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) is subject to that provider's own security practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
